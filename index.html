PTO V3

A simple PTO(Paid time off) calculator that uses how many years you have worked to get correct PTO rate. Then uses how many weeks you have worked so far this year to calculate total PTO then. Then deducts any PTO that you have taken so far Results can be returned in hours or days or weeks

  6. if years > 10 give PTO value of 7
  7. Delcare input for how many weeks they worked so far
  8. Process a equation to caclulate the paid time off so far
  9. Process a equation to caclulate how much paid time off they have left
  10. display selection
  11. Declare the options of weeks days and hours
  12. for weeks
  13. process weeks / 40.
  14. for days
  15. process days / 8.
  16. for hours
  17. process hours / 1.
